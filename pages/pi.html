关于闫老师About Prof. Yan

闫丽盈研究员现任职于北京大学第三医院生殖医学科，担任科研处处长及辅助生殖教育部重点实验室副主任。她是国家杰出青年科学基金获得者，长期致力于人类早期胚胎发育的遗传与表观遗传调控研究。 Prof. Liying Yan is a Principal Investigator at the Reproductive Medicine Center, Peking University Third Hospital (PUTH), where she also serves as Director of the Research Office and Deputy Director of the MOE Key Laboratory for Assisted Reproduction. She is a recipient of the NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar Award.

作为通讯或第一作者，她已在 Nature、Cell、Nature Genetics、Molecular Cell、Nature Communications 等国际顶级期刊发表论文45篇，他引超过2200次。研究成果两次入选"中国科学十大进展"，一篇论文荣登 Cell 杂志封面，三篇被评为 Web of Science 高被引论文。 As corresponding or first author, she has published 45 papers in top journals including Nature, Cell, Nature Genetics, Molecular Cell, and Nature Communications, with over 2,200 citations. Her work has twice been selected among China's Top 10 Scientific Advances, with one paper featured on the cover of Cell and three designated as highly cited by Web of Science.

她对学生倾注深厚的关怀，热衷于组织实验室团建活动，是学生们心中既严谨治学又温暖如家的好老师。 Known for her deep care for her students, she frequently organizes team activities and is cherished by lab members as a mentor who is both academically rigorous and genuinely warm.
