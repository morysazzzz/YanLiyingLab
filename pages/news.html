团队研究成果入选"2024年度中国科学十大进展" Team research selected as one of China's Top-10 Science Advances of 2024
北京大学乔杰、袁鹏、闫丽盈、魏瑗等从多维度揭示了额外X染色体影响男性生殖细胞发育的机制，为克氏综合征患者不育的发病机制及早期治疗提供了重要依据。研究成果于2025年3月27日在2025中关村论坛年会上获颁此荣誉。 The team's research on how extra X chromosomes affect male germ cell development in Klinefelter syndrome was recognized at the 2025 ZGC Forum as one of China's Top-10 Science Advances of 2024.

团队在 Advanced Science 发表胚胎诊断新方法 Team publishes novel embryo diagnostic method in Advanced Science
乔杰院士、闫丽盈教授、严智强副研究员团队开发出基于RNA转录组测序的胚胎诊断方法，可同步实现单基因病诊断与着床潜能评估，研究发表于 Advanced Science 期刊。 The team developed an RNA-based embryo diagnostic method enabling concurrent monogenic disease diagnosis and implantation potential assessment, published in Advanced Science.
