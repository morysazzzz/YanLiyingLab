团队在 Advanced Science 发表胚胎诊断新方法 Team publishes novel embryo diagnostic method in Advanced Science
乔杰院士、闫丽盈教授、严智强副研究员团队开发出基于RNA转录组测序的胚胎诊断方法，可同步实现单基因病诊断与着床潜能评估，研究发表于 Advanced Science 期刊。 The team led by Academician Qiao Jie, Prof. Yan Liying, and Associate Researcher Yan Zhiqiang developed an RNA-based embryo diagnostic method enabling concurrent monogenic disease diagnosis and implantation potential assessment, published in Advanced Science.
