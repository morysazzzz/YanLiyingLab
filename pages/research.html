研究方向Research

我们在探索什么What we explore

围绕人类早期胚胎发育与生殖遗传，我们的研究横跨基础科学与临床转化，致力于让更多家庭受益。 Spanning basic science and clinical translation, our work on early human embryo development and reproductive genetics aims to benefit more families.

方向一Area 1

生殖遗传与胚胎诊断Reproductive Genetics & Embryo Diagnosis

开发基于转录组测序的胚胎着床前遗传学检测（PGT）新方法，利用滋养外胚层细胞中丰富的mRNA转录拷贝进行突变检测，同步评估胚胎着床潜能，提高单基因病阻断的准确性与适用范围。 We develop novel preimplantation genetic testing (PGT) methods using transcriptome sequencing of trophectoderm cells to simultaneously detect mutations and assess embryo implantation potential, improving accuracy and applicability for monogenic disease prevention.

方向二Area 2

表观遗传调控机制Epigenetic Regulation Mechanisms

系统研究人类早期胚胎及原始生殖细胞发育过程中的DNA甲基化调控机制，解析卵母细胞减数分裂染色体重组的遗传特征，揭示表观遗传对基因表达的精准调控规律。 We systematically study DNA methylation regulation during human early embryo and primordial germ cell development, characterizing the genetic features of oocyte meiotic chromosome recombination, and revealing precise epigenetic control of gene expression.

方向三Area 3

辅助生殖技术临床转化Translational Assisted Reproduction

将基础研究成果成功转化为临床应用，包括胚胎植入前单基因疾病诊断、提高疑难不孕患者治疗成功率等，推动辅助生殖技术的精准化与个体化。 We successfully translate basic findings into clinical applications, including preimplantation diagnosis of monogenic diseases and improving success rates for difficult infertility cases, advancing precision and personalized assisted reproduction.
