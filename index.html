研究方向 Research

我们在探索什么 What we explore

聚焦人类早期胚胎发育、遗传与表观遗传调控，推动辅助生殖技术的科学突破。 Focusing on human early embryo development, genetic and epigenetic regulation, advancing breakthroughs in assisted reproduction.

🧬

生殖遗传与胚胎诊断 Reproductive Genetics & Embryo Diagnosis

开发基于转录组测序的胚胎着床前遗传学检测新方法，提高单基因病筛查的准确性与适用范围。 Developing transcriptome-based preimplantation genetic testing methods to improve accuracy and applicability for monogenic disease screening.

胚胎诊断 PGT
🔬

表观遗传调控机制 Epigenetic Regulation

解析早期胚胎及原始生殖细胞发育过程中的DNA甲基化调控规律，探索基因表达的表观遗传机制。 Deciphering DNA methylation regulation during early embryo and primordial germ cell development, exploring epigenetic mechanisms of gene expression.

表观遗传 Epigenetics
👶

辅助生殖技术转化 Translational ART

将基础研究成果转化为临床应用，提高辅助生殖治疗成功率，帮助更多不孕不育家庭实现生育梦想。 Translating basic research into clinical applications to improve ART success rates and help infertile families achieve their reproductive goals.

临床转化 Clinical Translation
